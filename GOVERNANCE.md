# Governance

Sakala Infra is part of Sakala, an open-source deployment project developed with transparent roadmap, public documentation, and community contribution.

## Project Stewardship

During the early MVP phase, Sakala is maintained by the initial maintainer team with support from GMEDIA as founding sponsor and infrastructure supporter.

## Sponsor Role

Sponsors may support infrastructure, domain, development resources, documentation, or community activities.

Sponsors do not automatically control technical decisions, roadmap priority, license changes, or contributor rights.

## Technical Decisions

Infrastructure contract decisions are made by maintainers through issues, pull requests, discussions, and documented roadmap updates.

## License

Sakala Infra is licensed under the Apache License 2.0.

## Future Governance

As Sakala grows beyond MVP and receives broader community contribution, the project may move to a dedicated community organization and adopt a more formal maintainer/governance model.
